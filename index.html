k8s@side8 $ cat training.md

Core Kubernetes concepts

Your engineers will play key roles in deploying and configuring a production ready Kubernetes cluster and re-platforming your applications onto it and will rapidly acquire a high level of Kubernetes proficiency.

Strong high level design patters

Your engineers will learn to use side8 and Kubernetes to orchestrate many classes of applications, including databases, queues, web applications, even external cloud components, allowing them to focus on adding business value insead of solving low level problems.

Site Reliability Engineering

Re-platforming applications onto Kubernetes implicitly teaches convergent systems, chaos engineering, designing for failure and managing distributed applications. Engineers will create more resilient systems on top of Kubernetes.

Rapid failure identification and resolution

During the re-platforming phase side8 periodically injects commonly seen errors into the stack, requiring engineers to briefly pause work and troubleshoot and resolve an issue in the cluster. Battle simulations equip your engineers with the knowledge to identify and resolve real issues they may see in the future.
